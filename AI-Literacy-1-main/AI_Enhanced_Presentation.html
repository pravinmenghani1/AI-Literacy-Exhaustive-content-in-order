💰 Real-World Example: Loan Prediction

Input Features → AI Decision

👤 Age: 30 years
Weight: W₁
💰 Salary: $190,000
Weight: W₂
🌍 Country: USA
Weight: W₃
⚧ Gender: Female
Weight: W₄
AI Formula:
Loan Decision = (W₁×Age) + (W₂×Salary) + (W₃×Country) + (W₄×Gender) + Bias

🤖 The AI learns the optimal weights during training to make accurate predictions!

🧠 Traditional ML vs. Deep Learning

📊 Traditional Machine Learning

Data Type: Structured (2D Tables)

| Age | Salary | Country | Loan |
|-----|--------|---------|------|
| 25 | 100K | USA | Yes |
| 30 | 190K | USA | ??? |

Examples: Spreadsheets, Databases, CSV files

🧠 Deep Learning

Data Type: Unstructured (3D+ Dimensions)

📸 Images: Height × Width × RGB
🎵 Audio: Time × Frequency × Amplitude
📝 Text: Thousands of dimensions
🎬 Videos: Height × Width × RGB × Time

🔑 When data gets complex and multi-dimensional, we need neural networks!
